E&C Traders with nowhere to go

Southwark Council and developer Delancey are making a song and dance about the small number Elephant & Castle shopping centre traders who have been allocated re-location space, but have kept very quiet about the 46 traders who haven't. With the help of LATIN ELEPHANT and SOUTHWARK NOTES we have recorded the voices of those traders whose businesses and livelihoods are being extinguished by the so-called regeneration of Elephant & Castle.
